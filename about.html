How Go Solar Sprouted

Go Solar helps homeowners realize their clean energy goals by creating customized packages that are both financially and environmentally beneficial. Since 2009, we have helped over 100,000 homeowners nationwide navigate the solar process through connecting our clients with the right $0 Upfront financing partner and the best local installer

Over 3 years ago, we relocated to the Sunshine State from San Diego, CA after seeing Florida’s potential to lead the solar industry’s progress. Since then we have grown to five locations in Florida as well as in Georgia, North Carolina, Alabama and growing. From your very first consultation to monitoring your system, our mission is simple: to make going solar simple.
